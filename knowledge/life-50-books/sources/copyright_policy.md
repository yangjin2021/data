# Copyright Policy

## Allowed

- Book metadata: title, author, year, category, short description.
- Links to legal public-domain or official resource pages.
- Short original notes written for this repository.

## Not Allowed

- Full text of copyrighted books.
- Scanned PDFs of copyrighted books.
- Pirated download links or mirror links.
- Long copied excerpts from copyrighted editions.

## Public Domain Caution

A work may be public domain in one jurisdiction and copyrighted in another. Translations, annotations, introductions, typography, and edited editions can have separate copyright. Always verify before redistributing text.
